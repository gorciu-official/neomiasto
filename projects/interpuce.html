AurorOS

Flagship product? I don't think so... But yeah, it's the most important project I have ever done. The golden age of AurorOS was its private beta. It looked like that shell in the Interpuce article thumbnail. Recently I have made a a bugfix release, so definitelly try booting it.

If you want to learn more about AurorOS, the best source is right there at the eclairbakery fandom (psstt... i made a shrine about eclairbakery right over here), I recommend you to Google Translate the site. At the time we even had a Wikipedia's page, but they quickly removed it.



Previous generation of AurorOS (a.k.a Interpuce/PreviousAurorOS)

This was the longest and the most reused AurorOS. The problem was that it had extreme, wrong codebase and functions that didn't work as expected, but they were left, because why not have a broken feature.

And it was at the time when ChatGPT evolved. I didn't understand most of the advanced code and I often used AI to write code. And the very same AI that was losing the big AI battle.
